beach song (demo) by Leonie Biney: next big thing?

If you're a fan of Lizzy McAlpine or Delaney Bailey, you'll definitely have to add this song to your playlist. Miss Leonie Biney posted a clip of this song she made on her TikTok account a while back and people loved it so much that she decided to release it, THANK THE LORD.

Why should I listen to this song?

Have you ever had a friend or significant other who has seemed to change over time and you're slowly growing apart and you know that you both feel it happening? This song perfectly and beautifully captures that feeling. Knowing you can't stop the impending death of a relationship, you just wish for one more day with them like it was before things started changing. Stuck hoping that one day you'll find your way back to eachother and things will go back to the way they were.

Leonie's voice is just the most gorgeous thing, as well. Have you ever just had that one person's whose voice you *cannot* get enough of? You can listen to their voice on repeat for hours and hours. She has that sort of voice and UGH-, it's so perfect.
